Problem Statement

Target Tracker

Design a robot capable of following a moving target across complex terrain, including uneven surfaces, slopes, and obstacles. The bot should use advanced sensors to track the target while navigating the terrain autonomously. The robot must dynamically adjust its movement speed and path to keep up with the target, even in environments where direct line-of-sight is frequently obstructed.
